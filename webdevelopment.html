See The Future Contracting - Website Design

This repository contains the website I designed and developed for See The Future Contracting, a licensed and bonded remodeling and handyman company serving Seattle, King, and Snohomish counties.

Role

Front-End Developer & Designer

Project Overview

The website was created to showcase the company’s professional remodeling, handyman, and deck construction services. It included pages for service descriptions, completed projects, testimonials, and a built-in estimate request form.

Note: The live website is no longer active. This repository serves as a portfolio example of my front-end development and design work.
